About Me

Thanks for stopping by!

My name is Catherine Jalomo, often referred to as Cat. I currently live in the Oak Lawn area of Dallas, Texas. I have lived in Dallas for 5 years now and have grown to love it. Prior to Dallas, I lived in San Antonio where I attended college (University of Texas - San Antonio & University of the Incarnate Word) and studied Architecture and Interior Architectural Design, respectively. After graduation, I interned as an Architect Intern at the City of San Antonio where I got to partake in some really cool projects in downtown San Antonio, primarily on the Riverwalk - it was great! We did lots of new construction, historic renovations, and land assessments. I then found a job with a local architect and we worked on government projects. We worked on renovations and new construction on ports of entry along the Texas border, new construction for schools in the San Antonio area, and military bases in San Antonio. I loved every minute of it but quickly found myself restless and began looking for something new.
